A Personal Advocate in Your Corner

We provide a safe, confidential environment to investigate the facts and advocate for your wellbeing during complex life transitions and institutional disputes.

Demystifying the System

Stormforge Hearth was founded to bridge the gap between everyday individuals and the rigid, often overwhelming bureaucracies they are forced to navigate. When dealing with things like school boards, government agencies, or difficult employers, the system is rarely designed to help you understand your rights.

We operate as your dedicated advocate and strategist. We listen to you, investigate the facts, outline your options clearly, and work alongside you to find a resolution that protects your interests and restores your peace of mind.

Our Wellbeing Approach

We do not rely on generic advice. Our methodology is rooted in professional advice, thoughtful investigation, and ethical advocacy. We look at the entirety of your situation, understanding exactly how the world is affecting you on a personal level.

By isolating the root causes of the conflict, we can build a structured, holistic path forward that empowers you to take back control.

About the Founder

Izak Tait built Stormforge Hearth to bring the rigorous advocacy and professional advice usually reserved for large organisations directly to individuals who need it most. With a background in ethics and investigations, and extensive experience navigating dense government bureaucracies, he knows exactly how to help you navigate complex systems.

Whether you require an advocate to work alongside you to resolve a dispute, or a quiet space to seek holistic guidance, Izak provides confidentiality, clarity, and a fiercely protective approach to your wellbeing.
